Tartarus

About This Project

A dark, stylized game project exploring the depths of creativity and challenge.

Tartarus is a game concept built to immerse players in a thrilling, atmospheric world inspired by mythology and horror elements.

The design draws heavily from deep reds, sharp contrasts, and minimal but impactful visual elements -- creating a sense of intensity and danger.

Players will explore, solve puzzles, and face challenges while experiencing a rich narrative filled with mystery.
